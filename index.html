Our Story

Founded in 2022, Clifford Furnitures Limited began as a small workshop in Nairobi with a vision to create high-quality, locally crafted furniture for Kenyan homes. Over the decades, we've grown into one of Kenya's premier furniture manufacturers while staying true to our roots.

We combine traditional craftsmanship with modern design techniques to create pieces that are both beautiful and functional. Every piece is crafted with attention to detail and built to last.
